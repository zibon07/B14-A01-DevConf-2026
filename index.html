Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Web Conference 2026

What is included in my conference ticket?

Your ticket includes access to all keynote sessions, workshops, networking events, and conference materials for the full duration of the event.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes, refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your ticket to someone else free of charge.

Will sessions be recorded?

All keynote and main-track sessions will be recorded and made available to ticket holders within 48 hours after the event.

Is there a virtual attendance option?

Yes, a Virtual Pass is available on the pricing page for anyone who cannot join us in person.

Do you offer group discounts?

Groups of 5 or more can contact our support team for special pricing before purchasing tickets.
